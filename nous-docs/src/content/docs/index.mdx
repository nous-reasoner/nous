## Why NOUS?

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Reasoning as Proof of Work" icon="star">
    Every block requires solving a 3-SAT formula — an NP-complete problem. Miners don't just hash, they **think**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI-Native Economy" icon="rocket">
    Built for minds that reason — human or AI. Intelligence yields competitive advantage over raw compute.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fair Forever" icon="heart">
    1 NOUS per block, forever. No halving, no premine, no cap. Every century produces ~21M NOUS.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simple by Design" icon="setting">
    One consensus rule, one difficulty parameter, one reward. Radical simplicity inspired by Bitcoin.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
